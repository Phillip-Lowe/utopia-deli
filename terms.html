📱 Text Message (SMS) Terms

By providing your mobile phone number on our order form or catering form, you consent to receive text messages from The Utopia Deli regarding:

  • Order confirmations and pickup reminders
  • Weekly meal prep menu announcements
  • Catering availability and event updates
  • Occasional promotions and community updates

Frequency

You will receive approximately 3 messages per week (Monday, Wednesday, and Friday) during meal prep campaigns. Additional transactional messages may be sent related to your specific orders.

Message Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan. The Utopia Deli does not charge for text messages.

Opt-Out Instructions

To stop receiving text messages: Reply STOP to any text message from The Utopia Deli. You will receive one final confirmation message and then no further texts.

To resubscribe: Reply START to any text message.

For help: Reply HELP or call (501) 551-5944.

📧 Email Terms

Opt-Out Instructions

To unsubscribe from emails, click the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email. Your request will be processed within 10 business days.
